Art

Traditional Art

I originally began my journey to actively improving my art at the age of 11. I had seen so many artists that I admired and had decided that I wanted to be able to do what they did.

Unfortunately, this journey was not a smooth one. During the majority of my High School years I recieved mass amounts of bullying for nothing other than trying to improve my passion. This had a very big weight on the confidence I had in my work, but I have since been able to rebuild this in the past few years.

Digital Art

Around the age of 14, I was bought my first digital drawing tablet by my parents. This rocketed me into the world of digital art and all of the creative freedom that came with it. I am still to this day improving and working on my digital art as you can always find room for improvement.
